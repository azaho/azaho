About

I earned my undergraduate degree and am currently a master's student at MIT in Computer Science and Brain and Cognitive Sciences. I am interested in the intersection of machine learning and neuroscience, and specifically in self-supervised feature learning using human neural data at scale to better decode and change the brain state across individuals. I am currently advised by Ila Fiete, and in the past by Guangyu Robert Yang. I co-founded Ukraine Leadership and Technology Academy with the goal to equip brilliant Ukrainian young leaders with the skills, knowledge, and confidence they need to rebuild Ukraine into a thriving, European nation. I won the Schwarzman Scholarship (class of 2026).
