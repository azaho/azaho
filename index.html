About

I earned my undergraduate degree and am currently a master's student at MIT in Computer Science and Brain and Cognitive Sciences. I am interested in the intersection of neuroscience and ML, especially self-supervised feature learning using human neural data at scale. I am currently advised by Ila Fiete, and in the past by Guangyu Robert Yang.

I co-founded the Ukraine Leadership and Technology Academy to equip brilliant young Ukrainians with the skills, knowledge, and confidence they need to rebuild Ukraine into a thriving, European nation. I also won the Schwarzman Scholarship (class of 2026).
