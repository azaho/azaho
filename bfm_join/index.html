[Seeking ML Intern] Foundation Models of Neural Activity

UPDATE: As of 6/15, the ML engineer position is now filled. However, if you're a believer in brain foundation models and would like to get into this research field, I really welcome that, and I will happily devote some of my time (when there's availability) to guide you through where we're at and current open problems that you could help us solve. Let me know if you would be interested! In addition, currently, a key goal of brain foundation models is to improve downstream brain decoding, and we have a benchmark (Neuroprobe) that is designed to quantify how well brain foundation models are able to decode information from the neural data such as speech, volume, pitch of heard sound, speaker identity, etc (just from brain data of people with electrodes in their brains!). We are looking into creating a challenge to improve brain decoding performance with a financial bounty (some thousand $) for the best models. Please send me an email if you're interested in either of the above. Best of luck! Best wishes, Andrii
